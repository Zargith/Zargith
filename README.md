## About me <img src="https://github.com/Zargith/Zargith/blob/master/Gifs/Hi.gif" width="30px">

Hi, I'm **Stanislas** 👋 — a polyvalent developer and pedagogical mentor at [Epitech](https://www.epitech.eu/), based in Mulhouse, France.

I graduated from Epitech in 2022 (Expert in Information Technology — RNCP Level 7) and spent my 4th year at [Keimyung University](https://www.kmu.ac.kr/uni/eng/main.jsp) in South Korea, specializing in video game development, 3D modeling, and animation.

Today, I combine my passion for development with a teaching mission at Epitech: mentoring students, creating pedagogical content, and building custom tools for training programs — all while keeping my skills sharp across the stack.

> *"A jack of all trades developer driven by insatiable curiosity. I love exploring new technical horizons, from code to infrastructure, turning my discoveries into concrete solutions."*
